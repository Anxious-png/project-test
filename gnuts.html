G. NUTS

G-nuts, or groundnuts, are the edible seeds of the Arachis hypogaea plant. They're native to South America and are widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
# Nutritional Value G-nuts are an excellent source of: 1. Protein (20-30%) 2. Healthy fats (40-50%) 3. Fiber (8-10%) 4. Vitamins E and B3 5. Minerals like potassium, magnesium, and iron

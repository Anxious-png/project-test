MAIZE

# Types of Maize 1. Dent maize: Most commonly grown for livestock feed and ethanol production. 2. Flint maize: Hard outer layer, often used for tortillas, tamales, and other traditional dishes. 3. Sweet maize: High moisture content, sweet flavor, and soft texture, perfect for human consumption. 4. Popcorn maize: Specialized variety that "pops" when heated. 5. Ornamental maize: Colorful, decorative varieties used for landscaping and fall decorations.


# Health Benefits 1. Antioxidant properties: Maize contains antioxidants that help protect against oxidative stress. 2. Supports healthy digestion: Maize's fiber content promotes regular bowel movements. 3. May help lower cholesterol: Soluble fiber in maize can help reduce cholesterol levels. 4. Supports bone health: Maize is a good source of several minerals essential for bone health.
